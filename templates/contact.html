Frequently Asked Questions
Common questions about MICT admissions and courses
How can I enroll in a course at MICT?
You can enroll by visiting our institute directly, calling us on +91 98765 43210, or filling the contact form above. Our team will guide you through the enrollment process.
Are the KLiC certificates recognised by employers?
Yes! KLiC certificates are issued by MKCL (Maharashtra Knowledge Corporation Limited) and are widely recognised by employers across Maharashtra and India.
What is the fee payment process?
Fees can be paid in cash at our institute or via online bank transfer. We also offer flexible installment options for selected courses. Contact us for details.
Do you provide placement assistance after course completion?
Yes, we provide career guidance and placement assistance to our students. We also conduct regular sessions on resume writing, interview preparation, and job opportunities.
Can I join a course in the middle of the month?
Yes, new batches start every month. You can join at the beginning of any month. Contact us to know the next batch start date for your preferred course.
Is there any age limit to join courses at MICT?
No, there is no age limit. We welcome students of all ages — from school students to working professionals and homemakers. Everyone is welcome at MICT!
