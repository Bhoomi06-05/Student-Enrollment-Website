✦ Who We Are

Maharashtra Institute of
Computer Technology

MICT Computer Education is a Government Recognised institution located in Maharashtra, India. We are an authorised centre for MKCL's KLiC (Knowledge Literacy for IT Careers) programs, delivering practical and industry-relevant computer education.

Our mission is to bridge the digital divide by providing affordable, certified, and quality IT education to every student — from beginners to advanced learners — with modern lab facilities and experienced faculty.
